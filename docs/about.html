About Us

About Us

At The Lovecats, we’re building a digital exhibition for everyone who loves cats and cares about their wellbeing. Our goal is to serve up emotionally and visually compelling, highly sharable cats-related pictures and introductions to as many people as possible to help make caring about cats a viral cause. We want our readers to fall in love with cats, have fun and be entertained while they’re doing it, and feel empowered to help animals in need. The Lovecats is for everyone who loves cats and wants to spread that love and make the world a better place for them.
